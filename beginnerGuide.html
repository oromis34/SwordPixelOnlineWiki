Beginner Guide

(a guide made from the Moonlit Black Cats)

Hello, reader. This is a quick small tutorial on the game for those that are having a difficult time understanding the game or how to play it.

We will start with the basics which will be understanding the menu.

MENU

You can open the menu by pressing the open menu button. It's pretty much the first button you see.

After this the MENU will open. Let's give a quick explanation of what the buttons are and what they do…

The first button is the account. It has 5 more buttons that are your stats, skills, equipment, items and cards (more on cards later).

The second button is Group & Friends. In there you can see your friend list, create a party and check on your guild. More on those later.

The third button is Messages… So far it does nothing.

The fourth button is Notifications. In there you can check the main quest and contracts (more on those later once again).

The fifth button is the Settings. There you can change your character customization. So far that is everything you can do.

The sixth button just closes the MENU.

MOVEMENT

Unlike other games where you would usually move around to get to other locations here you just have to press the GO TO BOAR FOREST button to move…

COMBAT

We finally got to the main part of the game. To start a fight all you have to do is press EXPLORE.


Once you get in a fight you will see the monsters you have to kill in order to come out alive. There is no way of fleeing a fight though so be careful with what you do. Anyways we will start with the basic features of combat. By now you might have a lot of buttons that you can click. I’ll give a quick explanation on them now.


OPEN MENU is self explanatory, so is attack. Skills opens a small UI that lets you select 5 skills that you have equipped. At level one you get Dark bash. You have to equip it through the skills menu before being able to use it though which you can do by opening menu > account > skills > select your skill and press one of the numbers. This can be done only outside of combat I think.

Anyways you have parry left and right, dodge left and right, block and switch and the 2 circles stamina and skillpoints.

Stamina is used on attacks, every type of blocks and some skills.

Skillpoints are used for most skills.

Switching is used for switching between the 4 circles which appear ONLY when you click on switch.


The reason you can go behind is because of parties. If you are behind a player you will not take any damage at all but you will have a -50% damage reduction. This is REALLY helpful when you are dealing with bosses because you can always fall back and heal up if you are not alone.

You can also select which mob to attack by simply clicking on them. You can see which monster you’ve selected by checking if they have a blue circle on them.


By now you might have noticed the purple hexagon (?) on the middle bottom of your screen. That's where you can see what attacks the mobs are doing. Lets see what you have to do when they attack you.

BATTLE SYSTEM AND COMMANDS

  • Right Attack > Right Parry: Press 'D' on your keyboard
  • Left Attack > Left Parry: Press 'A' on your keyboard
  • Right Charge > Left Dodge: Press 'Q' on your keyboard
  • Left Charge > Right Dodge: Press 'E' on your keyboard
  • Heavy Attacks > Block: Press 'W' on your keyboard
  • General Attack: Use the spacebar
  • Skills: Use keys 1-5 depending on the slot assigned to your skills.
